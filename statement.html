Artist statement

I make what I believe to be of value. I think the use of contemporary materials is justified in making contemporary art, however timeless art is beyond the material. Contrary to the popular notion of asking questions and not seeking answers, I prefer my questions to be answered.

Are we the programmers or are we the users?

We are the users of our own programs.

Do we have control over our programs?

Yes, to the degree that we understand them.

What is art?

It's a decision to act upon one's will and reveal it outside of oneself.
